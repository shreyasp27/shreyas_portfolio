ABOUT ME

I'm Shreyas Puttaraju, an AI Engineer specializing in the application of Generative AI and Machine Learning to solve complex business challenges. My experience spans developing innovative AI solutions, from spearheading rapid proof-of-concept applications using Azure OpenAI at Jewelers Mutual Group to engineering sophisticated multi-agent systems with LangChain and AWS Bedrock at PeritusHub.

My technical expertise lies in leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs), advanced prompt engineering, Python, and cloud platforms (Azure, AWS) to build impactful tools. I focus on enhancing automation, optimizing workflows, and driving efficiency, whether through AI-driven document processing (extraction, summarization, analysis) or pioneering novel agentic architectures for conversational AI.

During my time at H-E-B, I integrated computer vision with LLMs (Azure OpenAI, GPT-3.5-Turbo) via LangChain to extract product attributes from images, delivering measurable improvements. This experience, along with designing ETL pipelines, CI/CD processes, and robust evaluation frameworks, highlights my ability to manage the end-to-end development lifecycle of AI systems.

Driven by a passion for building practical and scalable AI solutions, I excel at translating complex requirements into functional applications. My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of generative AI, creating tools and systems that deliver significant value and streamline operations across diverse industries.
